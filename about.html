About Us

Our Mission

ABA's mission as a business organization is to develop well-rounded business leaders who put our skills to work and never compromise our humility, integrity, or passion for each other and the work that we do. As such, we provide our members with opportunities to gain hands on professional experience through various industry-focused projects.

Professional Development

Berkeley ABA prides itself on being both a business organization and a consulting club. We provide our members the opportunity to enhance their business acumen with our Consultant Development Program, in which our Cabinet and Alumni teach workshops in Excel, case analysis, market research, technical interviews, and more. Additionally, members learn about various fields of business by joining one of our five project teams and working on a semester-long consulting project with Fortune 500 companies. In doing so, our members are exposed to different areas of business and are given the skills.

Project Teams

Berkeley ABA members join one of our five project teams and work on a semester-long consulting project with Fortune 500 companies, gaining hands-on experience in various industries.

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

Business Development Consultants work with up-and-coming companies/start-ups with the focus in growth and sales strategies and product development.

FINANCE

Finance Consultants learn intermediate financial concepts through a detailed financial literacy program and provide financial & advisory services for clients.

MARKETING

Marketing Consultants learn the science of optimizing marketing campaigns and create comprehensive marketing strategies for large-scale companies.

NPO CONSULTING

NPO Consultants learn key business skills towards helping NPOs develop, providing consulting services for non-profits.

STRATEGY CONSULTING

Strategy Consultants study in-depth business casing frameworks to provide over-arching strategic recommendations for major corporations.
