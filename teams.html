Teams

Leadership

Cabinet Members

Cabinet Members within ABA are responsible for leading their team of consultants as they work on large, client based projects, while also providing necessary guidance and professional development for internships, jobs, and other opportunities. Cabinet Members include Officers, Project Managers, Senior Advisors, and Special Events Coordinator.

Senior Consultants

Senior Consultants within ABA focus on problem solving -- driven through dynamic team synergy, strong aptitude for learning, and creating innovative solutions throughout various project teams, while working closely with new and returning consultants to teach new skills.

Consultants

Consultants within ABA are dedicated to hands-on project work, actively driving project success. They thrive in collaborative environments, learning new skills while contributing to innovative solutions alongside their team members. With a focus practical application, Consultants play a vital role in supporting project teams and enhancing their expertise in the field.
