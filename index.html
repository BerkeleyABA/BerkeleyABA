Who We Are

Founded in 1975, Berkeley ABA has been transforming students into strong business leaders and providing Fortune 500 companies exceptional consulting services for 50 years. Within ABA, our members are guaranteed to find a tight-knit community, an extensive alumni network, and professional development curricula that trains our analysts to create impactful business recommendations for our clients such as Google, Airbnb, Microsoft, and more. Through hands-on client engagements, rigorous case training, and mentorship from experienced consultants, members gain the skills and confidence needed to thrive in any professional setting. Our legacy is built on a commitment to excellence, innovation, and a supportive culture that empowers every member to reach their full potential.

What We Offer

As one of UC Berkeley's premier business organizations, ABA provides incredible value for both its clients and members.
