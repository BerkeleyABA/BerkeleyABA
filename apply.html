Recruitment Timeline

Recruitment Week: 8:00AM - 4:00PM
Tuesday, January 21st — Sproul Plaza
We'll be tabling on Sproul Plaza everyday during Recruitment Week to meet potential applicants! Ask us questions, learn about the application process in greater detail, and pick up an informational handbill. This is the perfect time to come hangout to meet current members and alumni, as well as talk to us about our projects, cultures, and experiences!
Infosession: 8:00PM - 10:00PM
Tuesday, January 28th — SOCS 60
Come to our infosession to learn more about what Berkeley ABA is about! You'll have the chance to get in-depth information on our five project teams, organizational structure, and club opportunities. Make sure you stay afterwards to talk to our Project Managers to learn more about cultures, experiences, and more!
Case Workshop: 8:00PM - 10:00PM
Thursday, January 30th — SOCS 126
This is a great opportunity to go over the basics of casing, a skill that will definitely come up during the application process. Stop by for a chance to learn something new in a welcoming and unpressured environment!
Application DUE: 11:59 AM (before noon)
Friday, January 31st — Online
Fill out the application below. The application includes personal information, as well as questions to gauge interests, experiences, and personal fit. We also require documents such as a screenshot of your transcript and your resume in PDF form.
Interviews will be based on your application, so be sure to put effort into it. Successful applicants will be emailed Friday afternoon to attend our Group Interviews.
Interviews
Saturday February 1st - Sunday, February 2nd — *Invite Only*
Group interviews will take place on Saturday and will evaluate individuals teamwork, problem solving, and communication skills. Individual interviews will test technical abilities and holistic fit for the organization.
Social Night
Saturday February 1st — *Invite Only*
This is the perfect opportunity to come meet and get to know us on a personal level before individual interviews! The casual setting is a wonderful time to talk about personal passions and gauging if ABA is right for you. Feel free to drop by any time during the event. There'll be free food as well!
